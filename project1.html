Baltimore Black Techies - Community Events Page

HTML • CSS • Javascript/JQuery • Sketch • Adobe Photoshop

I am working with the Black Techies Meetup group to create a website for their organization. I am designing and coding the community events page where users can search for technology events in their area. Users can search for an event using keywords, their zipcode, and filter options such as topic, price and skill level requirements.
